实验室三篇论文分别被ICASSP和CHI 2026接收。<br>
论文《DIMO: DUAL-STRATEGY LEARNING FOR AMBIGUOUS SAMPLES IN CLASS-IMBALANCED FACIAL EXPRESSIONRECOGNITION》已被ICASSP接受、论文《SSG-DIT: A SPATIAL SIGNALGUIDED FRAMEWORK FOR CONTROLLABLE VIDEO GENERATION》已被ICASSP接受。与此同时，论文《Unveiling Road Rage Dynamics: Recreating and Modeling Road Rage in Audiovisual and Simulating Environments Based on Real-World Footage》被接收至CHI 2026（CCF A类会议）。<br>
